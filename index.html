Introduction.

As a tutor, I often find students encountering difficulty with the baisc fundamentals of math such as terminology, order of operations, multiplying/subtracting, solving the equation, scientific & exponential notation, and much more. Due to the vast amounts of concepts in Mathematics, these notes will only focus on a selected few.

Each lesson will consists of definitions, solved examples, and several pratice questions with solutions. I highly recommend you attempt these pratice questions before you take a look at the solutions. The purpose of this is to make mistakes and test if you really understand the concepts.

I hope each lesson will benefit you as you progress your journey in learning Mathematics.

Secret to Success!

Q: Have you ever wondered what makes a person smart?
A: People are not born smart, they work hard for it.

Intelligent people have 2 very important character traits;
1) Perseverance
2) Mindset

To achieve sucess and meet any of your goals, it is crucial that you have a strong work ethic & mindset! You need to put in time, effort, & focus if you want to make any of your dreams into reality!!

In life, it is OK to fail and not get everything right, nobody is perfect. The important thing is you learn from every failure. You use every expierence you encounter for growth. When you meet with failure, don't think with a "I can't do this" mindset. Think with a "How can I do this" mindset.
