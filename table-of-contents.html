Branches in Math.

The history and depth of mathematics is so huge that it can be branched into numerous areas. The main branches of Mathematics are;

  • Arithmetic — The branch of mathematics that focuses on numbers & basic operations (E.g Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division).
  • Algebra — The branch of mathematics that builds on after a foundation of arithmetic which focuses on variables & operations.
  • Geometry — The branch of mathematics concerned with the properties and relations of points, lines, surfaces, solids, and higher dimensional analogs.
  • Number Theory — The branch of mathematics devoted primarily to the study of the integers and integer-valued functions.
  • Analysis — The branch of mathematics dealing with rate of change, limits and related theories, such as differentiation, integration, measure, infinite series, and analytic functions. ( Note: Calculus falls into this category).
